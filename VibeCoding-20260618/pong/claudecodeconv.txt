● Clean. Here's what changed and how it works:

  Approach — the game still renders to a fixed 800×556 back-buffer every frame.
  Only the final blit to the window is new:

  1. ConfigureNotify events update win_w/win_h whenever the window is resized.
  2. blit_scaled() computes a uniform scale factor (min(win_w/800, win_h/556))
  so the game always fits without distortion.
  3. An XRender inverse-scale transform is applied to the source picture, then
  XRenderComposite does a bilinear-filtered blit into the window at the correct
  size and position.
  4. Black bars are painted in any unused margin (letterbox or pillarbox
  depending on the window's aspect ratio).
